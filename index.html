Vision & Mission

"Out-grow; Beyond, Incomprehensible to others."

Vision

To grow beyond college and create a meaningful venture in tech, education, or multi-domain creative projects, avoiding traditional corporate routes.

Sapphire aims to develop a strong identity through hackathons, workshops, and events, while maintaining authenticity, unity, and a close-knit culture.

Team Goal

Build a portfolio of impactful projects, such as LegalEase, and create opportunities for each member to contribute based on their strengths and grow together.

We value authenticity, creativity, and the unique talents each member brings to our collective journey.

Calestia

The Experimental Wing of Sapphire

Calestia is a handpicked trio within Sapphire, formed under a workshop assignment from the Head of the Department. It's a space where innovation, speed, and deep collaboration come together to deliver high-quality technical projects in short timelines.

This experimental wing serves as Sapphire's specialized task force, tackling challenging projects that require focused expertise and rapid development. The team combines complementary skills to create solutions that exemplify Sapphire's commitment to excellence.

Currently, Calestia is spearheading the development of LegalEase, our flagship project for automating legal document summarization and text simplification.
